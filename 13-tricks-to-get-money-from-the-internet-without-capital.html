Here are 13 Ways to Make Money on the Internet Without Capital

By: Technology Writing Team | July 07, 2025

"Apart from providing various information, the internet can also be used as a source of income. There are many ways to make money on the internet that can be done without capital."

Even though it is not something new, it is still difficult for some people to make money on the internet. It takes patience and hard work to achieve success.

There are many ways to make money on the internet, from small side income to large income that is quite promising for the future.

Income from the internet can be an additional monthly income, so it is important to set aside some of it to invest or save.

Business Opportunities on the Internet

Advances in information and communication technology have made it possible for people to work from anywhere, including from home. This has encouraged increased public interest in finding ways to make money on the internet.

Internet businesses offer a variety of opportunities for anyone with internet access. You can sell food online, become a freelancer in digital fields such as graphic design, or open an SEO consulting service.

Internet-based businesses also have the advantage of reaching a wider market. This is because the internet can be accessed by anyone, anywhere and at any time without knowing time and space.

Apart from that, internet-based businesses have flexible working hours. You can run this business at any time as long as there is an internet connection. This certainly provides freedom and flexibility for those of you who have other activities.

13 Ways to Make Money on the Internet

For those of you who are still beginners and have never made money via the internet, here are ways to make money on the internet that you can try:

  1. Blogging

    2. The first and quite popular way to make money on the internet lately is blogging. Through blog content, you can reap quite large profits.

    You can make money from blogging by signing up for AdSense. Once AdSense is active, your blog will display advertisements that will be paid every time a visitor clicks on the advertisement.

    By creating good and quality content you will attract the attention of many readers. However, how to make money on the internet through blogging requires patience in building a website.

  2. YouTube

    3. Apart from blogs, another popular way to make money on the internet is through YouTube. All you have to do is create a YouTube channel and upload videos of your work regularly and consistently.

    YouTube video content that you can make includes food reviews, daily life vlogs, unboxings, talk shows, tutorials, and even live streaming while playing games.

    However, you have to monetize by allowing YouTube to include advertisements in the videos you create as a way to make money on the internet. You will get paid for every click on a YouTube ad.

  3. Affiliate

    4. One way to make money on the internet is with affiliates, where you offer other people's products or services. If you successfully sell one product or service, you will get a commission.

    The more people who make purchases through affiliates, the more money they make. Isn't it quite a promising way to make money on the internet by becoming an affiliate?

  4. Taking Paid Surveys

    5. Taking or filling out paid surveys can be one of the easiest ways to make money on the internet without capital at the moment.

    You will usually get points, where each point collected will receive different rewards depending on company policy. There are companies that provide shopping vouchers, cash or credit to people who take surveys.

  5. Dropship

    6. This way of making money on the internet by selling goods from other sellers is popular among novice business people. Because, a dropship business does not require capital to start.

    If there are buyers who are interested in the goods offered, you can simply contact the seller of the goods and ask him to send them to the consumer. You can increase the selling price slightly to make a profit.

  6. Freelancers

    7. Another way to make money on the internet is to become a freelancer. If you like using social media networks, don't forget to register on a website that collects freelancer portfolios.

    A freelancer can work from anywhere using a smartphone or laptop. One example of freelancers who are much needed are contributing writers and others.

  7. Influencers

    8. If you have a lot of followers on social media, there's no harm in trying to make money on the internet by becoming an influencer. You will get additional income by opening endorsements on your social media accounts.

  8. Entrustment Services

    9. Entrustment services (jastip) are one way to make money on the internet that is widely used. This service offers convenience for people who want to buy goods from abroad or other areas that are difficult to reach.

    The method is quite easy, namely by buying the goods that the buyer wants to sell at a price higher than the purchase price. This price difference is an advantage for you.

  9. Resellers

    10. Becoming a reseller is one way to make money on the internet that is quite popular. Resellers are people who buy products from suppliers of goods, then resell them at higher prices.

    Resellers do not need to make their own products, so it is suitable for beginners. As long as you have the will and hard work, anyone can become a successful reseller.

  10. Content Creator

    11. The profession of content creator in the current digital era is increasingly popular and is in great demand by various groups, especially the younger generation. You can take advantage of this opportunity as a way to make money on the internet.

    Even though they are often considered the same as influencers, a content creator can create content about various things, from hobbies, lifestyle, to social issues. In contrast, influencers usually create content that promotes products or services.

    The main task of a content creator is to create content in the form of tutorials, vlogs, reviews, or just entertainment that is interesting and informative for their audience. Here are some tips for creating interesting content:

    1. Choose the type of content that suits your abilities
    2. Determine your content style
    3. Analyze the target audience
    4. Create content that is unique and different from the others
    5. Provide information and education in your content
    6. Be diligent in creating content consistently
    7. Promote your content on various social media
    8. Stay updated with current trends

    With creativity and hard work, you can earn additional income from becoming a content creator.

  11. Podcaster

    12. Podcasts are a form of entertainment that is currently popular. Audio content that is packaged in an interesting and informative way can be entertainment for listeners, as well as a source of income for podcasters.

    Podcast income is influenced by the number of followers and the number of listeners. The more followers and listeners your podcast has, the higher the income you will earn.

  12. SEO Services

    13. If you have skills and knowledge about SEO (Search Engine Optimization), you can offer SEO services to business people as a way to make money on the internet.

    SEO is the process of increasing a website's visibility in search engines. With SEO, a website will be easier for potential visitors to find, thereby increasing traffic and sales.

    Don't forget to promote your services so that potential clients can find out about them. You can promote SEO services through social media, websites, or freelancer websites.

  13. Sell ​​Photos and Videos

    14. For those of you who have a hobby of photography or videography, selling your photos and videos can also be a way to make money on the internet that doesn't require capital.p>

    Photos and videos are visual content that can be used for various purposes, such as commercial, editorial or personal purposes. With good quality, you can sell it on a special image provider platform.

So, those are some ways to make money on the internet that you can do according to your abilities. With hard work and persistence, you can earn quite a large additional income from the internet.
